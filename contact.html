Openings

We are actively recruiting talented undergraduate students, graduate students and postdoctoral researchers! Please directly contact Prof. Li by email. Individuals with expertise in one or more of the following areas are especially encouraged to apply.

  • Optoelectronics
  • Synthesis and characterization of conjugated materials.
  • Biomaterials and bioelectronics.
