Research Focus

Wearable sensor | Molecular electronics | Edge computing | Optoelectronics

Research Areas

Wearable Sensor

Developing intrinsically stretchable devices and circuits for human-machine interactions. Our soft, skin-like wearable sensors enable the continuous, high-fidelity acquisition of multimodal physiological data, paving the way for non-invasive and personalized health monitoring.

Molecular Electronics

Elucidating charge transport mechanisms at the single-molecule level using molecular characterization techniques. We focus on the rational design and precision synthesis of sequence-defined oligomers to establish robust structure-function relationships for next-generation organic electronics.

Edge Computing

Developing neuromorphic architectures and hardware-software co-design strategies to enable intelligent, on-body data processing. By integrating machine learning algorithms directly into wearable circuits, we achieve low-latency, energy-efficient edge computing for real-time data analysis.
